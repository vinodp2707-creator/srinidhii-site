About

Srinidhii Sreeram is a Bharatanatyam dancer whose artistry is marked by a strong command of rhythm, a nuanced approach to abhinaya, and an energetic stage presence. A senior disciple of Smt. Padmini Krishnamurthy, she began her training at the age of six and has since performed widely across India, the Middle East, the United Kingdom, and the United States. Beyond performance, Srinidhii is an engaging teacher who brings patience, joy, and creativity into her classes. Her teaching blends discipline with joy, encouraging students to discover their strengths while nurturing a deep respect for the art form. Now based in London, Srinidhii continues to expand her practice as both performer and educator, seeking to share Bharatanatyam as a living dialogue between tradition and today’s world — an art form that resonates across cultures and generations.
