About Srinidhii Sreeram

Srinidhii Sreeram is a senior disciple of Kalaimamani Smt. Padmini Krishnamurthy, Director Sri Saraswathi Gana Nilayam, Chennai & Sri Saraswathi Natyalaya, Muscat. Beginning at age six, she has nurtured her craft through numerous recitals as part of the institution.

Her Arangetram took place in July 2013 in the august presence of Late Guru Ranganayaki Jayaraman and Smt. Nandini Ramani. Since then, she has presented solo and group works in India, Muscat, Dubai, the USA, and the UK. Audiences and mentors recognise her strong grasp of rhythm, expressive abhinaya, and energetic stage presence.
