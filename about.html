Srinidhii Sreeram is a senior disciple of Kalaimamani Smt. Padmini Krishnamurthy, Director of Sri Saraswathi Gana Nilayam, Chennai, and Sri Saraswathi Natyalaya, Muscat.

She presented her Arangetram in July 2013 in the august presence of Late Guru Kalaimamani Ranganayaki Jayaraman and Smt. Nandini Ramani. Since then, Srinidhii has delivered numerous solo and group recitals across the globe including in India, Muscat, Dubai, the United States, and the United Kingdom earning recognition for her rhythmic precision, expressive abhinaya, and commanding stage presence.

Most recently, she was invited to perform at the prestigious Cleveland Thyagaraja Festival (USA), where her performance received widespread recognition and critical acclaim.

Now based in London, Srinidhii continues to explore and share the timeless beauty of Bharatanatyam, blending tradition with her own artistic voice through performances and teaching.
