Srinidhii Sreeram is a senior disciple of Kalaimamani Smt. Padmini Krishnamurthy, Director Sri Saraswathi Gana Nilayam, Chennai & Sri Saraswathi Natyalaya, Muscat. Having begun her journey in the Indian classical art form at the age of 6, Srinidhii has since nurtured her craft and has been a part of numerous recitals staged as part of the dance school. Srinidhii gave her maiden performance (Arangetram) in July 2013 in the august presence of Late. Guru Kalaimamani Ranganayaki Jayaraman, and industry stalwart Smt. Nandini Ramani. She has since given various solo and group performances regularly in India, Muscat, Dubai, and USA. Most recently, she was invited to perform at the prestigious Cleveland Thyagaraja Festival, USA and has been recognized for her strong grasp of rhythm, expressive rendition of abhinaya, and energetic performances.

  • Over the years, Srinidhi continued a holistic education in dance and completed all 6 levels of the Prayathnam Dance Examinations. In 2022, She was conferred the title - ‘Yathna Praveena’
  • In recognition of her art, she was awarded the ‘Young Artist Grant for Performing Arts’ from the Government of Tamil Nadu and has been recognized as a Graded Artist by the Government of India.
  • Srinidhii began her teaching journey early on and has been instrumental in training students as part of the dance institution. Her style of teaching involves a knack for understanding her student’s strengths, a fun yet focussed approach to learning, and creating a space for creative exploration for children and adults alike
